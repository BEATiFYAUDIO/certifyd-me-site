What you actually own

Ownership is concrete in Certifyd. These are the four things that stay under creator control.

Identity

Your creator identity and verification history stay attached to your profile, not trapped in one platform account.

Content

You publish from your own workspace with contributor attribution and release context preserved.

Commerce

Buyer payments, receipts, and unlock states are linked to your releases with clear transaction evidence.

Revenue

Splits and payouts are tracked as distinct states, so you can see what was earned, paid, and still payable.

How it works

A simple flow from release to payout.

1. Creator

You create your identity and set your release terms.

2. Publish

You publish content with contributor roles and shares attached.

3. Buyer

A buyer opens your release and starts checkout.

4. Payment

Payment is recorded with receipt and entitlement proof.

5. Split

Earnings are distributed to creator and collaborators by the release split.

6. Payout

Payouts execute automatically with visible paid and pending states.
